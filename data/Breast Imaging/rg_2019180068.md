Figure 2. Chart depicts the diagnostic algorithm used for BI-RADS category 3 lesion management with follow-up at 6, 12, and 24 months. A BI-RADS category 3 lesion should be updated to a category 2 if the lesion disappears or appears benign at 2-year follow-up. If the lesion size increases by 20% during follow-up, a BI-RADS category of 4 should be assigned.
