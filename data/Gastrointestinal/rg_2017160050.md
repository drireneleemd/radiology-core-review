Figure 2. Images obtained with three different imaging modalities in a 29-year-old woman with IBD. *A,* CT and, *B,* MR enterographic images show mesenteric vessel engorgement with fibrofatty proliferation (white arrows) and fat stranding (curved arrow). Note the segmental small-bowel wall thickening (black **\***) associated with fistulous sinus (white **\***) that directs toward the mesenteric abscess (arrowheads). *C,* Image from anteroposterior small-bowel follow-through examination showss the terminal ileum with both transverse and longitudinal ulcerations (black arrows). Note oral contrast material extraluminal leakage toward the abscess (arrowheads).
